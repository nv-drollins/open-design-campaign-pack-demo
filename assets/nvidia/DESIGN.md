# NVIDIA SIGGRAPH Demo Design Direction

Use assets/nvidia/BRAND-NOTES.md as the brand-usage source of truth.

Use only official NVIDIA assets found in assets/nvidia/.
Do not recreate, recolor, distort, crop, stylize, or apply effects to the NVIDIA logo.

Visual direction:
- dark premium workstation studio
- precise typography
- NVIDIA green as a restrained accent
- generous negative space
- technical but not gamer/cyberpunk
- designer remains the art director; assistant supports exploration and critique
