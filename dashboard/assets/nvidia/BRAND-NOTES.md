# NVIDIA Brand Notes

Source: https://www.nvidia.com/en-us/about-nvidia/legal-info/logo-brand-usage/

Use only the official NVIDIA logo files provided in this project.

Logo rules:
- Do not redraw, recreate, stylize, distort, recolor, crop, or apply effects to the NVIDIA logo.
- Use the full two-color logo when possible.
- Keep strong contrast against the background. NVIDIA specifies a "minimum contrast ratio of 1:10".
- Preserve clear space around the logo. Minimum clear space should be "at least the height of the n".
- Do not place the logo on busy backgrounds.
- Do not imply a partnership, endorsement, or sponsorship beyond the intended demo context.
- Do not use third-party logo-site artwork, fan art, or unofficial NVIDIA imagery.

Design direction:
- Backgrounds may use dark, technical, premium, high-performance visual language.
- NVIDIA green should be an accent, not a full-page wash.
- Keep the logo as a separate clean asset layer.
- Generated backgrounds must not contain logos, text, or NVIDIA-like marks.

Product context:
- This demo is about DGX Spark as a local AI design-assistant workstation.
- The assistant helps designers explore layouts, copy, variants, and critique.
- The designer remains the art director.
